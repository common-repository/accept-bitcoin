Accept Bitcoin payments in WooCommerce. Easy to use. No registration, no middleman, no trusted third party.

== Description ==

Simple, easy-to-use payment gatway for WooCommerce that lets you accept Bitcoin payments.

Just enter your wallet address, and click activate. No registration, no middleman, no trusted third party.

== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Do I need to share my private key? =

No, you will only have to enter your wallet address to use this payment gatway.

= Do I have to register at some third party? =

No, this plugin does not require you to interact with any third party.
